Frequently Asked Questions

What are NDIS Line Items & Pricing?

We adhere strictly to the latest NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits. All charges are transparent and discussed upfront before signing service agreements.

Is my child eligible for ECEI?

Children aged 0-6 with a developmental delay or disability are generally eligible. You do not need a formal diagnosis to access initial early childhood support.

What is the benefit of Plan Management?

Plan Management gives you the flexibility to use both registered and unregistered NDIS providers without the headache of handling invoices and payments yourself.
