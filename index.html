Our Heritage
Defining Care.

Care and Care started with a simple vision: to simplify the NDIS journey for families. We understood that navigating developmental delays and disability support can be overwhelming. Today, we stand as a premium provider, committed to delivering high-end, personalized care that genuinely transforms lives.

Our Services.

Early Childhood (ECEI)

We work closely with children aged 0-6 years who have developmental delays, building confidence through proven play-based strategies.

Support Coordination

Navigating the NDIS can be overwhelming. We help you understand your plan, connect with the right providers, and maximize funded supports.

Daily Activities

Compassionate support to help you live independently. Our trained staff assist with everyday tasks in the comfort of your own home.

Plan Management.

How It Works

We act as your personal NDIS accountants. You choose the providers you want (registered or unregistered), and we handle the invoices, record-keeping, and portal claims.

Pricing Structure

Our fees are strictly in line with the latest NDIS Pricing Arrangements. There are no out-of-pocket expenses for you.

  • Set up costs covered by NDIS
  • Monthly processing fees covered by NDIS
  • Complete transparency through our dedicated portal
